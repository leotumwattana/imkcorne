The IMK Corne is a premium case designed to upgrade, protect and accentuate the beautiful outlines of one of Japan's most unique custom split keyboard: the Corne "Helidox" by foostan.

The plate and protective side walls are machined from a single block of aircraft grade aluminum. Giving you a case that is both light enough for travel and strong enough to prevent flex.

The case features an open bottom design for use with Corne's acrylic bottom plate. Allowing the under-glow LEDs to shine through whilst also giving you a window to inspect the beautiful PCB and components inside.

The tenting holes has been carefully placed to allow the case to lay flat for travel when the tenting legs are removed.

You have four M3 sized tenting holes that you can use to adjust both the tent and tilt of the keyboard to your liking.

You can use the anodized black tenting legs that comes with the case, or simply replace or join your choice of readily available M3 standoffs and screws for unlimited angles and style.
